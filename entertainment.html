Kylie Padilla confirms knowledge of Aljur Abrenica and AJ Raval’s children

MANILA, Philippines — Actress Kylie Padilla has finally spoken up after AJ Raval publicly revealed that she and Aljur Abrenica have three children together.

In a Facebook post on Wednesday, Padilla said she has long known about the matter and chose to put the children’s welfare first.

"This will be my only comment to put an end to this. I’ve known about it for a long time, but of course, we prioritized the welfare of the children. They’re very close, and that’s what’s most important," she wrote.

"I’m happy that now they don’t have to hide anymore. Proud of you. peace all around. I hope the drama ends,” Padilla added.

Her response came hours after Raval's emotional interview on “Fast Talk with Boy Abunda," where the 25-year-old actress confirmed long-standing rumors about her and Abrenica having children together.

“Actually, Tito Boy, I already have five kids,” Raval said, revealing that her eldest is a seven-year-old daughter named Ariana, followed by her late son Aaron. Her three other children — Aikina, Junior, and Abraham — are with Abrenica.

Raval said she decided to speak up to end the speculation and allow her children to live freely.

“I just want this to be over, and I want my kids to have their freedom,” she explained.

“At first, it was difficult [to be a mother], but my family has always been there to support me. I managed to get through it," she added.

The actress also revealed that Abrenica's sons with Padilla, Alas and Axl, share a close relationship with her own children. She described Alas as a “very responsible” older brother.

Padilla and Abrenica tied the knot in 2018 and parted ways three years later. Meanwhile, Raval and Abrenica first met in 2021 when they worked together on the film "Nerisa."
